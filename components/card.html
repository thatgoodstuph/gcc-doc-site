Card

Container component for grouping related content and actions. that communicate state, quantity, or category.

Overview

Cards are small visual indicators that provide additional context or information. They are typically used to show status, counts, or categories.

Usage

When to Use

  • Display status information (new, updated, active)
  • Show counts or quantities (notifications, items)
  • Indicate categories or tags
  • Highlight important information

When Not to Use

  • For primary actions (use buttons instead)
  • For long text content (use labels or text instead)
  • As standalone navigation elements

Variants

Card variants include status badges, count badges, and category badges. Each serves a specific purpose in the interface.

Do & Don't

Do

  • Keep badge text concise (1-2 words)
  • Use appropriate colors for status badges
  • Ensure badges are readable at small sizes

Don't

  • Overuse badges - they should add value, not clutter
  • Use badges for critical information
  • Make badges too large or prominent
